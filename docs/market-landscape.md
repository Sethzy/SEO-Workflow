# AI Search Visibility Market Landscape

This repo sits in the overlap between classic SEO operations and the newer
AEO/GEO category: making brands discoverable in Google, AI Overviews,
ChatGPT-style answers, Perplexity-style research, and other answer surfaces.

## What The Market Is Funding

The category is no longer only "write more blog posts." The strongest companies
are building operating systems for visibility: monitor how models talk about a
brand, find content and citation gaps, create or revise content, route review,
and measure whether visibility improves.

## How SEO Workflow Maps To The Market

SEO Workflow is a workflow engine for the content operations layer:

1. Discover seed topics and exclude already-published keywords.
2. Use Ahrefs data to validate search demand and difficulty.
3. Score keywords by business value, traffic potential, intent, and difficulty.
4. Run SERP analysis and convert the winning opportunities into briefs.
5. Generate publication-ready MDX through a specialized SEO/AEO/GEO skill.
6. Add internal links and validation gates before commit.

The strongest market mapping is not "AI writes posts." It is:

- search research becomes a repeatable workflow
- briefs are evidence-backed instead of vibes-based
- content generation carries brand, SEO, AEO, and GEO rules
- humans stay in the loop at the Ahrefs and review gates
- validation catches AI-writing tells, SEO misses, and answer-engine structure

## Truth Boundary

This repository is a public workflow artifact. It contains the runbook, agent
steps, and quality rules. It does not claim production traffic gains, revenue,
rankings, or customer outcomes unless those are added later with source evidence.
